## Body Section Color Strategy

### Cuerpo (Front Body)

The most visible area - this is your primary team color.

<Tip>
**Best Practices:**
- Use your team's signature color
- Choose colors with good camera presence
- Avoid very light colors that show dirt/sweat
- Medium to dark tones work best for main body
</Tip>

### Espalda (Back)

Often matches the front, but can create interesting designs when different.

**Creative Options:**
- Match front for classic look
- Use complementary color for dynamic design
- Lighter shade than front for subtle variation

### Mangas (Sleeves)

**Strategic Choices:**
- **Contrasting Sleeves:** Make arms more visible (important for streamers)
- **Matching Sleeves:** Clean, unified appearance
- **Gradient Effect:** Use shade between body and collar

<Note>
Bright sleeve colors help with motion visibility in gameplay footage and streaming.
</Note>

### Cuello (Collar)

**Options:**
- **Accent Color:** Small pop of team's secondary color
- **Neutral:** White or black for classic sports look
- **Matching Body:** Seamless integration

## Typography Best Practices

The platform offers 4 fonts: **INTER**, **ACTOR**, **MONTSSERRAT**, **JOMHURIA**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="INTER - Modern Sans-Serif">
    **Character:** Clean, contemporary, highly readable
    
    **Best For:**
    - Tech-focused teams
    - Minimalist designs
    - Teams wanting modern, professional look
    - Small text that needs clarity
    
    **Pairs Well With:**
    - Blue and teal color schemes
    - Sleek, minimal logos
    - Corporate sponsor branding
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="ACTOR - Bold Display">
    **Character:** Strong, impactful, attention-grabbing
    
    **Best For:**
    - Team names that need to stand out
    - Aggressive, competitive branding
    - Short team names (2-8 characters)
    - Headers and main titles
    
    **Pairs Well With:**
    - Red and orange designs
    - Bold geometric logos
    - High-contrast color schemes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="MONTSSERRAT - Geometric Sans-Serif">
    **Character:** Balanced, professional, versatile
    
    **Best For:**
    - Classic sports team aesthetic
    - Medium-length team names
    - Professional esports organizations

    **Pairs Well With:**
    - Navy and white schemes
    - Traditional logo designs
    - Any color combination
    
    <Tip>
    Montserrat is the safest choice - works with almost any design.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="JOMHURIA - Stylized Display">
    **Character:** Unique, decorative, memorable
    
    **Best For:**
    - Teams wanting distinctive identity
    - Creative/artistic team brands

### Font Pairing Strategy

You can select different fonts for team name and player name:

**Recommended Combinations:**
- **Team:** ACTOR, **Player:** INTER (Bold title, readable names)
- **Team:** JOMHURIA, **Player:** MONTSERRAT (Unique team, classic players)
- **Team:** MONTSERRAT, **Player:** MONTSERRAT (Cohesive, professional)
- **Team:** INTER, **Player:** INTER (Modern, unified)

<Tip>
When in doubt, use the same font for both - consistency is professional.
</Tip>

## Logo Upload Guidelines

### File Specifications

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Recommended Format">
    **Best Format:** PNG with transparent background
    
    **Why:**
    - Transparency adapts to any uniform color
    - No white boxes around logo
    - Professional appearance
    
    **Alternative:** High-contrast SVG or PNG with solid background
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Resolution Requirements">
    **Minimum:** 300 DPI at final print size
    
    **Recommended Dimensions:**
    - Width: 1000-2000 pixels
    - Height: 1000-2000 pixels (square ratio best)
    
    **File Size:** Under 5MB for optimal upload performance
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Design Considerations">
    **Logo Design Tips:**
    - Simple logos read better from distance
    - Avoid fine details (may not print well)
    - High contrast between elements
    - Test visibility on your chosen uniform colors
    
    <Warning>
    Gradients and subtle shading may not reproduce accurately on fabric.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Logo Placement Strategy

**Common Placements:**
- Center chest (most visible)
- Upper left chest (classic sports position)
- Back center (large format)
- Sleeve patch (secondary branding)

<Tip>
Provide high-resolution versions even if upload interface accepts lower quality - the production team may contact you for better files.
</Tip>

## Team Branding Consistency

### Creating a Cohesive Identity

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define Core Colors">
    Choose 2-3 primary colors that represent your team:
    - Primary: Main body color
    - Secondary: Accent color (sleeves/collar)
    - Tertiary: Optional highlight color
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Signature Font">
    Pick the font that matches your team personality:
    - Competitive/Aggressive: ACTOR
    - Professional/Modern: INTER or MONTSERRAT
    - Creative/Unique: JOMHURIA
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Design Consistent Logo">
    Ensure logo:
    - Uses team colors
    - Matches font style personality
    - Scales well at different sizes
    - Works on all uniform color combinations
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Test Combinations">
    Before finalizing:
    - View design on different backgrounds
    - Check readability of text
    - Ensure logo contrast is sufficient
    - Consider how it looks in photos/streams
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Player Details Best Practices

### Jersey Numbers

<Tip>
**Number Selection Tips:**
- Use 0-99 for standard recognition
- Avoid numbers that look similar when flipped (6/9)
- Consider player positions or roles in-game
- Famous/retired numbers create recognition
</Tip>

### Sizing Strategy

**Available Sizes:** S, M, L (for Hombre/Mujer/Niño categories)

**Ordering Tips:**
- Order sample sizes before bulk order
- Consider shrinkage in first wash
- Account for layering (undershirts, etc.)
- Different genders may fit differently

### Quantity Planning

**Recommendations:**
- Order 1-2 extra uniforms per player (backups)
- Consider practice vs. competition uniforms
- Account for team growth
- Bulk orders may have minimum quantities

<Note>
The system tracks quantity per player, allowing different amounts for starters vs. substitutes.
</Note>
